The process

Four steps.
Zero back and forth.

No proposals. No negotiation. No three-day wait for a reply. Pick, book, confirm. Done in under sixty seconds.

01

Browse and pick

Search by service, city, tier and price. Every profile is manually reviewed by our team before it goes live. No fake portfolios, no misleading claims.

02

Book the slot

Pick a fixed package, choose a date and time, pay securely into escrow. Confirmation lands in under sixty seconds. No back and forth required.

03

They show up

GPS-verified check-in on every onsite booking. Your creator arrives when they said they would. If they do not, every rupee comes back to you immediately.

04

Approve and pay

Deliverables come through Qritr. You review everything. You approve when satisfied. The creator gets paid only then. That is what escrow actually means when enforced.
